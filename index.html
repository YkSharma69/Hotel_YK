About Our Hotel

The History of Our Hotel

Yk Sharma’s Hotel was founded in 2022 with a bold vision: to redefine affordable luxury for travelers across India. What started as a personal dream by Mr. Y.K. Sharma quickly grew into a nationwide hospitality brand, now proudly located in cities across the country.

Great Location

No matter where your journey takes you, Yk Sharma’s Hotel is never far away. With a presence in cities across India, we’re always right where you need us — whether you're traveling for business, leisure, or a little bit of both. Our hotels are strategically located near major transport hubs, business centers, shopping areas, and local attractions, so you spend less time commuting and more time enjoying your trip. Whether you're in Delhi, Mumbai, Bangalore, or any other city, you’ll find us in the heart of the action. We make sure every location is easy to find.

Meals On The House

At Yk Sharma’s Hotel, we believe that good food makes a great stay even better. That’s why we offer complimentary meals to all our guests — because comfort shouldn’t stop at the room. From hearty breakfasts to homely dinners, our meals are freshly prepared with quality ingredients, served with care, and always included at no extra cost. Whether you’re grabbing a quick bite before a busy day or winding down in the evening, we’ve got you covered.

Fitness Room

At Yk Sharma’s Hotel, your wellness matters — even when you’re on the go. That’s why our fully equipped fitness rooms are open to all guests, giving you the freedom to keep up with your fitness routine, your way. Whether you're into cardio, strength training, or a quick morning stretch, our gyms offer modern equipment in a clean, comfortable space. Early riser or night owl? No problem. Our fitness centers are accessible 24/7 in select locations, so you can work out when it works for you.
